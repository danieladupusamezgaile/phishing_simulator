How to spot phishing attempts:

1. Check the sender's email address

Legitimate organizations use professional email domains that match their names.

2. Look for spelling and grammar errors

Professional emails rarely contain obvious errors.

3. Be wary of urgent or threatening language

Phishers often create a sense of urgency to make you act quickly without thinking.

4. Hover over links before clicking

Check if the URL matches the legitimate website you expect.

5. Never provide credentials on unexpected login requests

Instead, navigate directly to the service by typing the address in your browser.
